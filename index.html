About Us

Who are we and what do we provide?

Located just five minutes from JFK Airport, we are a fully bonded Container Freight Station offering Certified Cargo Screening services. Our facility specializes in transportation solutions to and from JFK, EWR, and across New York City’s five boroughs and the Tri-State area.

With nationwide reach, we support businesses with tailored transportation services, sourcing the best rates to meet your operational and budgetary needs.
